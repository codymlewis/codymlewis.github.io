I'm a researcher studying the security, fairness, and privacy concerns within machine learning. My goal is to better understand machine learning and intelligent systems, in order to improve their safety and broaden their utility. I mainly have expertise in machine learning and deep learning, cyber security and trust, and secure systems and protocols.
